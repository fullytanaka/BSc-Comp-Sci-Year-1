Induction Materials

  • Induction Materials

    Welcome to the Department of Informatics! 

    This section houses all the induction materials to help you settle into your course and life at King's.

    As well as the information on this page, please also read the  Welcome to King's pages  as they contain all the steps you need to go through before you join and for getting Started. You will also find details of the Virtual Welcome Hub, giving you the chance to explore the variety of services offered to support you through your journey at King's. 

    Please do take your time to read through all the information on this page carefully and we will answer any questions you have during our live question & answer sessions.

    We are trialing a new automated captioning system so there may be some errors in the video captions. If you do spot any errors, please email details to ug-informatics@kcl.ac.uk so that we can update them accordingly.

    Welcome from Luc Moreau, Head of Department
